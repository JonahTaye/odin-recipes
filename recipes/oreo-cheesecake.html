Oreo Cheescake

Description

Oreo cheesecake is a decadent dessert that combines the rich, creamy texture of traditional cheesecake with the beloved crunch of Oreo cookies. The base is often made by blending crushed Oreos with butter, creating a perfect crust that complements the smooth filling. The cheesecake itself is made with cream cheese, sugar, and eggs, creating a dense yet velvety texture.
